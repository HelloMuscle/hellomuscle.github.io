Who is Sylvie?

Meet Sylvie Lestideau RMT, an experienced and compassionate Registered Massage Therapist. She graduated with honours and received the Heart Award from the 3000-hour program at VCMT. Sylvie's diverse background, shaped by various life experiences, gives her a unique perspective on well-being.

As an RMT, Sylvie is committed to meeting clients where they are and appreciating them unconditionally, fostering a warm and positive regard. Your well-being is a journey that Sylvie is prepared to take with you as you are ready.
